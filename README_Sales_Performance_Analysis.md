# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data to identify revenue
trends, top-performing products, regional profitability, and overall
business performance.

The objective is to transform raw sales data into actionable business
insights using an interactive Power BI dashboard.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🎯 Business Objective

To help the business answer key questions:

-   Which products generate the most revenue?
-   How do sales change over time?
-   Which regions and categories are most profitable?
-   Where should the business focus to improve growth and profitability?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🛠 Tools Used

-   **Power BI** (Data Modeling & Dashboard Development)
-   **Power Query** (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
-   **DAX** (KPI Calculations)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📂 Dataset

Superstore Sales Dataset\
Includes: - Order Date - Category & Sub-Category - Region - Sales -
Profit - Product Name - Order ID

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🧹 Data Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and prepared using Power Query:

-   Checked and handled missing values
-   Corrected data types (Date, Numeric fields)
-   Removed duplicate records
-   Created time-based columns (Year, Month)
-   Built calculated measures using DAX

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

-   **Total Revenue:** 271.73K\
-   **Total Profit:** 50.68K\
-   **Total Orders:** 624\
-   **Profit Margin:** 18.65%

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1️⃣ Revenue Trend

-   Sales show noticeable growth toward the end of the year.
-   Q4 demonstrates strong seasonal demand, indicating festive or
    year-end purchasing behavior.

### 2️⃣ Category Performance

-   Technology leads in both revenue and profit.
-   It is the primary driver of overall business profitability.

### 3️⃣ Regional Performance

-   East and West regions generate the highest profits.
-   South region contributes comparatively lower profit, suggesting
    potential improvement areas.

### 4️⃣ Product Performance

-   A few top products significantly outperform others.
-   Revenue appears concentrated among high-performing products.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1.  Increase inventory and marketing efforts before Q4 to maximize
    seasonal sales opportunities.
2.  Invest more in the Technology category due to its strong revenue and
    margin contribution.
3.  Evaluate pricing and operational strategy in lower-performing
    regions.
4.  Reduce dependency on a few top-performing products by expanding
    complementary product promotions.
5.  Review cost and discount strategies to further improve overall
    profit margin.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📊 Dashboard Features

-   KPI Summary Cards
-   Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
-   Sales by Category (Column Chart)
-   Regional Profit Analysis (Bar Chart)
-   Top 10 Products by Sales
-   Interactive Filters (Year, Category)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

This dashboard provides a clear, business-focused overview of sales

It enables data-driven decision-making by highlighting growth drivers,
